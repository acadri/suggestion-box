Help Center

Find answers to common questions about the suggestion system

What is the Student Suggestion System?

+

The Student Suggestion System is an official platform for Muni University students to submit ideas and feedback to help improve various aspects of university life.

How do I submit an effective suggestion?

+

Be specific about the issue, provide context, offer practical solutions, and maintain a constructive tone for the best results.

What is the review process?

+

Suggestions are reviewed by relevant departments, evaluated for feasibility, and you'll receive updates throughout the process.
